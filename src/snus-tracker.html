Snus Tracker App: Take Control of Your Habit

A snus tracker helps you consume more consciously. Breezer makes tracking simple, visual, and social — ideal for anyone who wants to understand their snus habits before deciding to reduce or quit.

Why use a snus tracker?

Most snus users underestimate how much they consume each day. Once you log every pouch, the real picture becomes clear. A snus tracker doesn't just count pouches — it shows trends over weeks and months so you can spot patterns you'd never notice otherwise.

With Breezer, you log each snus with one tap, see how long it's been since your last one, and get a reminder when you hit your personal daily limit. That's the difference between guessing and knowing.

Snus tracker vs. quit app

Classic quit apps push you to stop immediately. Breezer starts with awareness and self-control. You decide whether to reduce or switch to Quit Mode when you're ready. That flexibility is what makes Breezer useful for different goals at different stages.

Who is the snus tracker for?

Breezer is built for snus users who want control without judgment. Whether you're trying to understand your habit, cut back gradually, or just see the numbers — the tracker adapts to your pace.

Users consistently report that simply making their consumption visible leads to fewer pouches per day. Add in social rankings and friends, and tracking becomes motivating rather than a chore.
