Why use a Zyn tracker?

Nicotine pouches feel effortless, which is exactly why consumption creeps up unnoticed. A Zyn tracker makes the real numbers visible — how many pouches a day, what it costs, and how your nicotine intake stacks up week to week.

Breezer logs each pouch with a single tap, shows your trends in charts, and reminds you when you hit your daily limit. Stay in control without having to quit right away.
